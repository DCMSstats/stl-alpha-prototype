Apply online to register a new property as a short term let in England

Short term letting is when a residential property is rented out (or let) for a period of less than 90 nights.

This registration can be completed by:

  • an individual owner and operator
  • a multiple owner and operator (on the property with others)
  • a Limited Liability Company (LLC)
  • a Management agency
  • combination of the above

Before you start

  • You’ll need proof of compliance certificates.
  • Check if your dwelling type needs to be registered

Continue your application

If you've already started to apply, you can continue your application

Cost

Each application costs £45 per property. You can pay by MasterCard, Visa, Electron or Delta debit or credit card.

How long it takes

You’ll usually get a response within 5 weeks from submission.
