Overview of short-term let registration scheme in England

Short-term letting is when a residential property is rented out (or let) for a period of less than 90 nights.

! Warning Operating without a short-term let licence is a civil offence. From 1st January 2026 you will need to prove your registration status to list your property on booking platforms.

Registration includes confirming that you are complying with the necessary regulations.

Once registered, you will receive a registration number and digital badge that you can display when advertising your property. The requirement to register short-term let properties will ensure that all short-term let operators know about, and meet, the required safety and planning regulations.

This is a new service, and available in non digital format, if you wish to receive the hard copy of the registration form please contact: example@stlregisteration.gov.uk
