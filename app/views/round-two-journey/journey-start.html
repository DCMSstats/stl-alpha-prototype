Apply online to register a new property/ properties as a short-term let in England

Short term letting is when a residential property is rented out (or let) for a period of less than 90 nights.

Who qualifies to complete the short-term registration:

  • Owners of a single short-term let property
  • Owners of multiple short-term let properties
  • Registering on behalf of a property owner
  • Management agency

How much will it cost

Each application costs £45 per property. You can pay by MasterCard, Visa, Electron or Delta debit or credit card.

How long it takes

You’ll usually get a response within 5 weeks from submission.
